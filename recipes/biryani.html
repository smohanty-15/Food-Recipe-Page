Chicken Biryani

Recipe Info

Cooking Time: 1 hour

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (washed and cut)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaves, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Oil/ghee and salt to taste

Steps to Cook

  1. Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes and boil till 80% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste for 1 hour.
  3. Fry onions till golden brown and set half aside for garnishing.
  4. In a large pan, cook marinated chicken with tomatoes and spices until done.
  5. In a deep pot, layer rice and chicken. Top with fried onions, mint, and coriander.
  6. Cover with a lid and cook on low flame (dum) for 20 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with raita or salad.
