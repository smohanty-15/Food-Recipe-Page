Chicken Biryani

← Back to Home

🕒 Cooking Time:

1 hour

🥘 Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala)
  • Onions, garlic, ginger, mint
  • Salt & oil

👨‍🍳 Steps to Cook:

  1. Marinate chicken with spices and yogurt for 30 mins
  2. Cook rice 80% and drain
  3. Fry onions and cook chicken
  4. Layer rice and chicken, garnish with mint
  5. Cover and cook on low flame for 20 mins
