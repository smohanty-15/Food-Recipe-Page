Creamy Pasta Recipe

Recipe Info

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 2 people

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Steps to Cook

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté garlic until golden.
  3. Pour in the cream, stir continuously.
  4. Add boiled pasta and mix well.
  5. Season with salt, pepper and add cheese if desired.
